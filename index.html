      I have two channels featured in youtube, they are Ra-Ri Channel and Ra-Ri Clips

      Out of all the Projects, I feel like Youtube is the biggest one.

      Then in 6th grade, just before August 2021, I made a video called,Nutritious Milkshake for a school project. But to send it there, Whatsapp will be a difficult option and so, my father helped me release that video on my mother's account. Then my father found out that all the entertaining videos can be sent to youtube as memories and thus Ra-Ri Channel is born.
